Harmony of a Hunter - Harmony of Shinesparkers

Harmony of a Hunter

2011

Harmony of a Hunter was our debut album created to celebrate the twenty-fifth anniversary of the Metroid franchise. The album features thirty-six tracks across all Metroid titles released at that time, in a variety of different styles.
